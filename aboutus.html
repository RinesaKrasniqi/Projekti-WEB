CEO of My Dental Care

Dr.Smith

Specialist in Odontology for the past 20 years. Dr.Smith is a
board-certified dentist Odontology specialist providing general anesthesia,
intravenous sedation and pain management services for patients of all ages
requiring restorative dentistry or maxillofacial surgery in a private office setting.
His goal is to provide safe, compassionate, hospital-quality care,
tailored to the patient's specific needs and medical history, while fostering
a peaceful and calm environment.


Dr.Smith loves his job, which perfectly combines his passion for science
and enthusiasm for quality patient care. Dr.Smith is a dedicated family man.
In his free time, Dr.Smith enjoys all that sunny California has to offer: surfing,
hiking, and sandy beach days with his wife and son.

Doctor at My Dental Care

Dr.Armuela

Works as a Dental hygienist or Dental therapist.I strive to offer culturally
empathic healing insight for each client that I meet. My approach is client centered
and I meet my clients and their families where they are at developmentally,
emotionally,and experientially. I work with my clients from a whole person approach,
and support them in whatever way that they need, in order to create a space where we can utilize our time together to address whatever is holding them back and ultimately, to heal. I have great faith in the audacity of the human spirit to reveal what it needs to in time, and to be ready to receive and celebrate the emerging gifts of each client's experience.

I believe that the relationships in my client's life matter in their work with me. I believe
that cultural empathy is a real skill, and being able to hold space for clients in this way
is particularly important to me.
